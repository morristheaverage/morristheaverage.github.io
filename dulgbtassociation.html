Join Us

Membership of The Hill Orchestra is open to any member of Durham University and costs just £12 per year. Being a member has many perks, such as discounted Hill Orchestra merchandise, biscuits at rehearsals, and recognition of your hard work through inclusion in the concert programmes.

Alternatively, if you have any questions, feel free to contact us at the society email address.
